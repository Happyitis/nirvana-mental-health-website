Mental Health Conditions

Mental health conditions affect many people across the world. Understanding these conditions can help individuals manage their symptoms and get the appropriate help. Below are some common mental health conditions.

Anxiety

Anxiety disorders are characterized by excessive worry, fear, and stress. They can affect a personâ€™s daily life and relationships.

Depression

Depression involves persistent feelings of sadness, loss of interest, and low energy. It affects how people feel, think, and handle daily activities.

PTSD

PTSD is a mental health condition triggered by experiencing or witnessing traumatic events. It causes flashbacks, nightmares, and severe anxiety.

OCD

OCD involves unwanted, recurring thoughts (obsessions) and repetitive behaviors (compulsions) that interfere with daily life.

Bipolar Disorder

Bipolar disorder causes extreme mood swings, including emotional highs (mania) and lows (depression), affecting a person's energy and activity levels.

ADHD

ADHD is characterized by difficulty focusing, hyperactivity, and impulsive behaviors, affecting both children and adults.
