Find Support

At Nirvana, we provide a variety of ways to connect with support. Choose the option that suits your needs the best, and let us help you through your journey to mental wellness.

How Can We Help You?

Chat with a Volunteer

If you need someone to talk to, our trained volunteers are available for live chat. This option allows you to express your thoughts and emotions in a safe and anonymous space.

Start Chatting

Call a Therapist

Speak directly to a certified therapist over the phone. This option is perfect for those who prefer verbal communication and need real-time advice or counseling.

Call Now

Find a Local Therapist

Want a face-to-face session? Use our directory to find licensed therapists near you. Set up an appointment for personalized care that fits your schedule.

Find a Therapist

Emergency Support

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, please use these emergency services:

  • Call Emergency Services: 100
  • Suicide Prevention Hotline: 16600102005
  • Text for Help: Text HELLO to 1166
